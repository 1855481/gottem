Numbers Divisible by 2

What are numbers divisible by 2? A number is divisible by 2 if it has a 0, 2, 4, 6, or 8 in the ones place. It is additionally considered even if it is divisible by 2 with the outcome of a whole number.

In a coders perspective: If you are aware of what the modulo operator is (%), it functions almost the same way when dividing a number by two to test whether or not it's even. The modulo operator tests to see whether or not the left hand operand is divisible by right hand operand while stating if there's a remainder or not.
